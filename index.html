About Us

Sahyadri Agro Industries was started in 2015 by three friends with the vision of Producing the premium quality cashew nuts. Sahyadri Agro Industry is cashew producing industry based in Chandgad, Kolhapur. This region of Konkan is gifted by the mother Nature, well known for its beautiful seashores and excellent climatic conditions for Cashew farming. In Sahyadri Agro Industry, we use such best quality raw cashews as raw material. All processing is done considering the International guidelines to produce the best of it. We process various types and grades, please check Products for more information. We focus on Standard process, Best in Price product and excellent service.

Our vision is to supply authentic Konkan cashews to a new and emergent market throughout the world by providing the premium quality cashew products.

We started with Mission to produce the premium quality cashew nuts and introducing customers with the authentic Konkan cashew nuts.

Throughout this period, Sahyadri Agro Industries has become the undoubted leader in authentic cashew producing sector.

We are known for Authentic Konkan cashew manufacturing, best quality cashew products, excellent service and great marketing network.
